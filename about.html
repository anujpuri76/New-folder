M r. Sanjeev Sagar founded Agrosaw in 1984 with the goal of offering the Indian agricultural sector effective and cutting-edge post-harvest solutions.
Early on, Agrosaw faced a number of difficulties, including a lack of funding and farmers who were ignorant of contemporary post-harvest technologies. However, the business persisted and put a lot of effort into becoming recognized as a trustworthy and cutting-edge provider of post-harvest equipment.
The dedication of Agrosaw to quality and customer satisfaction enabled the business to overcome these difficulties and win the confidence of its clients.
When Agrosaw unveiled its first domestic grain-cleaning machine, it achieved one of its major turning points. Customers liked this product, and it helped make Agrosaw a dominant player in the Indian market.
Agrosaw has developed and expanded its product line over the years to include a variety of post-harvest machinery and tools. Additionally, the business has established a substantial network of dealers and distributors throughout India and abroad, guaranteeing that its goods and services are easily accessible to clients.
The company continues to build on its history of innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. Today, Agrosaw is acknowledged as one of India's top manufacturers of agricultural machinery and a leader in providing solutions.
