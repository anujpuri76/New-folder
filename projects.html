Turnkey Solutions



A grosaw's capable of delivering high quality and safe solutions for your grains and seeds. Our aim is to increase efficiency, improve traceability and reduce loss. We deliver complete point solutions for Seed &grain of all crop types by Plant engineering.

Our capability to design & engineer individual machines has been further extended to designing complete solutions for its customers by integrating a number of machines to build a complete and efficient solutionfor customer’s plant.

Agrosaw undertake plant designing for: Conveying, Cleaning, Drying, Grading, Milling, feed mill, Malting, Storage and handling of seeds, grains, spices, pulses, Cocoa, Coffee, oil seeds, fruits and vegetablesetc.

We assist our customers by increasing efficiency, productivity, and yield. The manufacturing operation require the movements of men, machines and materials. Generally, few inputs tend to be static while the others are moving.

We prepare & provide complete product and process layout, generally the machines have fixed installations, and the operators are static in terms of their specified workstations.

Objectives of Agrosaw Projects:

1. Minimization of material handling.
2. Elimination of bottlenecks through the balancing of plant capacities.
3. High material turnover through a shorter operating cycle.
4. Effective utilization of installed capacity so that the returns on the investments may be maximized.
5. Effective utilization of cubic space in the factory area.
6. Effective utilization of manpower resources through the elimination of idle time.
7. Elimination, improvement or confinement of objectionable operations e.g., operations with bad odour, vibrating operations etc.
8. Elimination of physical efforts required by operative workers.
9. Avoidance of industrial accidents.
10. Better working conditions for the employees like lighting, ventilation, control of noise and vibrations etc.
11. Advising customers on plant design to ensure the best and optimum efficiency levels.
12. Customizing, designing, manufacturing and commissioning the entire plant.
13. Handing over of the entire plant to the customer.
14. Training the customer's staff in plant operations.
15. Providing after sale and maintenance service 24/7.

Agrosaw's key competency lies in the ability to understand and implement customer requirements inbuilding plants and provide desired quality of end products.

24/7availability, Our Team is made available to resolve the issues and provide solutions for customer.

Agrosaw also provides in best plant automation as per customer and solution requirements.
