Installation & Commissioning Training





    

A grosaw provides installation & commissioning services to its customers.
The service can be on purely supervision basis whereby Agrosaw
engineers supervise the customer's installation/commissioning team or it can be
entirely on turnkey basis where Agrosaw undertakes the complete responsibility
for execution, including provision of labour & material.

The service includes:-

1. Constructing foundation for the equipment

2. Assembly and erection of machines

3. Providing electrical connections to equipment from main panel using required cable size

4. Trial of the installed machine after installation

5. Agrosaw serves its customers worldwide, through well qualified & trained installation teams.

6. Electrical Layout, Physical Layout (Foundation Drawings) also provided as a service.

Real-Time Remote Assistance & Collaboration

                                                           

Training:Agrosaw provides complete training to customer's staff for proper operation and maintenance of the installed machine at the time of commissioning. Agrosaw also organizes in house training for the customer's staff to augment their technical know-how about the machines and its operation.
